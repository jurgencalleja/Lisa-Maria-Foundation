The Board of Administrators

Tony Zahra

Tony Zahra is the father of Lisa Maria Zahra Fenech who died tragically on 19th March 2014.

Tony is an entrepreneur and also the Honorary Consul General for the Republic of Lithuania in Malta. He has served as a council member on a number of constituted bodies including the Federation of Industry, MHRA and held various appointments in Government Agencies.

Tony is also the Chairman of the Alpine Group and as part of the Corporate Social Responsibility, the Group financed the construction of a day care centre for children in Ethiopia.

Winston Zahra

Winston J. Zahra is the Chief Executive Officer of Island Hotels Group Holdings plc.

After graduating with a First Class Honours in Hotel and Catering Management, Winston spearheaded the development of Island Caterers Limited into becoming one of Malta’s leading event catering companies. Since then, he has also been extensively involved in the growth of Island Hotels Group Holdings plc including the Coastline Hotel, the Radisson Blu Resort St. Julian’s and the Radisson Blu Resort & Spa Golden Sands together with the launch of the Costa Coffee brand.

Mary Anne Lauri

Studied at the University of Malta, the London School of Economics and read for a PhD at the University of London. Joined the Department of Psychology at the University of Malta in 1992 and is presently Associate Professor and Pro-Rector. Has co-authored several publications, and published in both local and international journals. Is a chartered member and chartered scientist of the British Psychological Society.

Joe Borg

Joseph Borg is the Administrative Co-Ordinator of The Lisa Maria Foundation.

He has spent a long career in the management of various companies in the private sector where originally he started his career as an accountant with the Alpine Group and after thirteen years moved on to work in general management capacities with other companies operating in a variety of commercial activities. In 1998 he obtained, with Distinction, an MBA from the Maastricht School of Management of the Netherlands. He is now retired but dedicates a good part of his time to support voluntary organisations in their administrative, operational and other management functions.
